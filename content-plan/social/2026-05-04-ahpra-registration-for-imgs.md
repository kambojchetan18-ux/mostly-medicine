# Social drafts — AHPRA Registration for International Medical Graduates: 2026 Step-by-Step Guide

## LinkedIn (Amandeep)

If you've just passed the AMC exams and you're now staring at the AHPRA portal thinking "what comes next?" — I've been there. That moment when you realise the exam was the easier part.

I've watched over 136 IMGs navigate this pathway, and I can tell you the biggest myth: that AHPRA processing is slow. It's not. The bottleneck is almost always document completeness. One missing letter from your medical school, one good-standing certificate that takes twelve weeks to arrive, and suddenly you're looking at a nine-month application instead of four to six months.

Here's what actually matters:

• **Know your pathway.** If you're from India, Pakistan, the Philippines, Nigeria — you're on the Standard pathway. That means provisional registration first (intern year under supervision), then general registration. If you're from the UK, Canada, US, Ireland, or New Zealand with substantive registration there, you might qualify for the Competent Authority pathway and skip straight to general registration.

• **Collect everything before you start.** Your primary medical degree certificate, transcripts, internship completion letter, good-standing certificate from your home medical council (this one takes 4-8 weeks — request it first), employment history, English language test results. Missing one document cascades into weeks of delay.

• **EPIC verification is the real timeline driver.** Your medical school and postgraduate institution need to respond to ECFMG's verification requests. UK and US institutions: 2-4 weeks. India and Pakistan: 10-16 weeks. Call your institutions proactively the day you submit your EPIC request. This single action cuts weeks off the process.

• **English language test must be current.** IELTS, OET, PTE Academic, or TOEFL iBT — results must be within three years and sent directly from the testing body to AHPRA. OET is most common for Commonwealth-trained doctors; PTE returns results in five business days if you're under time pressure.

The realistic timeline from EPIC verification request to registration grant is 4–6 months. That's doable. But it requires you to move first, not wait for AHPRA to ask.
